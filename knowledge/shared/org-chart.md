## How to Read Ownership

- **Vertical Director**: Sets strategy, owns OKR, directs all PMs in the vertical
- **PM**: Owns a product area end-to-end (discovery → delivery → GTM)
- **TPM**: Owns execution tracking and cross-team coordination (not product strategy)
- **Matrixed**: Some PMs span multiple verticals (e.g., Abhishek owns both Ecosystem and Custody Platform areas)
