## File Naming Convention

`{firstname}-{lastname}.md` — all lowercase, hyphen-separated.

Examples: `akshay-thakur.md`, `jane-doe.md`

## What Belongs in a Brain File

See `framework/templates/brain.md` for the full template. The brain file captures:

- **Role & scope**: vertical, PM level, team size
- **OKRs**: current cycle objectives and key results
- **Active initiatives**: what you're building right now
- **Key partners**: external relationships you own
- **Team**: who you work with and what they do
- **Open items**: what needs decisions or follow-up
- **Recent decisions**: what was settled and why
- **Cross-functional dependencies**: who you depend on / who depends on you
- **Context dump**: everything else that matters for context

## What Does NOT Belong

- Personal immigration status, visa applications, or legal proceedings
- Compensation, performance review notes, or HR matters
- Personal appointments, medical information, or family details
- Anything you would not share in a company all-hands
